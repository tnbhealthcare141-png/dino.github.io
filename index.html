Business Problem

TNB HealthCare, which serves staff, retirees, and dependents, needs a more efficient support experience to reduce customer frustration and improve service access.

Current Issue: High Call Abandonment Rate

Many customers end their calls before speaking to a customer service representative because of long waiting times, repetitive enquiries, and delays in getting immediate support.

  • Long waiting queues during peak hours
  • Insufficient customer service agents
  • Slow call routing to the right department
  • Repeated common enquiries taking agent time
  • Customer frustration and reduced confidence

Business Impact

  • Lower customer satisfaction and weaker trust in service quality
  • Higher number of complaints and poor service perception
  • Loss of business opportunities due to unresolved calls
  • Heavier workload for human agents
  • Higher operational costs and negative brand reputation
134,000 Members TNB HealthCare serves staff, retirees, and dependents.

1,240 Panel Facilities Panel hospitals and clinics accessible through the mobile app.
Core Services GL requests, claims, wellness, referrals, and support enquiries.

AI Solution

The proposed Smart AI Voice Assistant acts as the first point of contact for customers, giving immediate responses through voice and chat before routing complex cases to live agents.

About the AI Assistant

This AI assistant is designed for TNB HealthCare customer support. It can answer common enquiries, collect customer details, guide users on healthcare-related benefits, and transfer complex issues to the appropriate live support team.

  • Provides immediate response without queue waiting
  • Answers common healthcare support questions
  • Supports complaint and issue collection
  • Routes customers to the correct support team
  • Can be enhanced with ElevenLabs voice interaction

TNB HealthCare Information Used

  • Guarantee Letter services management
  • Claims management for panels and members
  • Wellness management initiatives
  • Klinik TNB access and locations
  • Medication Delivery Services (MDS)
  • THC Mobile App and guidebook support
  • Benefits and member engagement support
